How to Play the Game


  1. Aim of the Game:

    Your goal is to guess the secret number chosen by the computer.

  2. Getting Started:

    The game will randomly select a number between 1 and 100. You will be presented with an input field where you can enter your guess.

  3. Making a Guess:

    Enter your guess in the input field. Click the "Submit Guess" button to submit your guess.

  4. Feedback:

    After submitting your guess, the game will provide feedback indicating whether your guess is too low, or too high.

  5. Keep Guessing:

    Keep guessing until you correctly identify the secret number or reached the maximum attempt of 10.
